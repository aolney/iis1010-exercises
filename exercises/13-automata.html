Learning Automata

We previously saw how the Game of Life could generate complex behavior from simple rules.

This lab has measurements from the Game of Life:

  • The current status of a cell (True=Alive; False=Dead)
  • The next status of a cell
  • The number of neighbors of a cell (0 to 8)

We have a huge list of these observations in the `data1` block.

Try calculating the **4** different frequencies associated with 2 neighbors.
